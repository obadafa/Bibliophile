True Stories

Reading changed the lives of millions of people for the better.

I used to feel trapped in my small town, with nothing exciting ever happening. Then, I discovered fantasy novels. They took me to magical worlds with dragons, elves, and brave heroes. Reading became my escape, but it was more than that. It sparked my imagination and showed me possibilities beyond my everyday life. Now, I'm writing my own fantasy novel, inspired by the stories that transported me to different realities.
Amer

Cairo, Egypt

"I was stuck in a dead-end job, feeling unfulfilled. One day, I picked up a biography of a successful entrepreneur. Their story of overcoming obstacles and achieving their dreams resonated deeply with me. Reading it inspired me to take control of my own life. I quit my job, started my own business, and I'm now on a path to achieving my goals. That book was a turning point for me."
Omar

Khartoum, Sudan

Reading has been my refuge during difficult times. When I lost a loved one, I found solace in poetry and memoirs about grief. The words resonated with my pain and offered a sense of comfort. Reading also helped me find humor during dark times. Lighthearted novels or funny essays provided a much-needed escape and lifted my spirits.
Ammar

Damascus, Syria
